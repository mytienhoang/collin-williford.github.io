Running Now In College

Now that I'm in college I am no longer part of a team so I decided to join the running club here at UNCC last year. We don't take things as serious at a college team would and I mostly participate just for the fun of it. I still find it enjoyable to stay in shape and continue to challenge myself competitively at both cross country and track meets that clubs host.

The picture above is from the track meet that our club went to recently that was hosted by the running club down at University of Georgia. We ourselves are about to host a meet on 3/26 which we will have 7 other clubs travel to our track for a track meet. Right now we are in Outdoor track season so most of the popular events tend to be mid distance like 800m,1600m, or 3200m and I personally have been enjoying competing in the 1600m recently.
