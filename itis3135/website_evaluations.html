Website Evaluations

IBM

This is IBM's home webpage for all its customers. It allows access to their products, solutions, consulting and customer support.

  • Contrast - Good, mostly black text with white background with a royal blue elements added through the site.
  • Repitition - Great, buttons and interactive aspects of the site stay consistant without overloading the front page
  • Alignment - Great, table of contents on left hand side allows for easy navigation
  • Proximity - Great - elements are well spaced out and allows user to easily see what they are looking at

CraigsList

This is the homepage for craigslist which acts as a table of contents for almost anything you would need in your local area

  • Contrast - Bad, home page consist of white background with almost all of the text being hyperlink blue
  • Repitition - Bad, site has a lot of text that is easy for quick navigation but looks terrible
  • Alignment - Okay, page doesn't utalize all of the screen so you change the size of the tab everything just stays perfectly still
  • Proximity - Bad, all of the text is a small font size and lowercase so everything is forced to be real close to each other and makes it hard to read without zooming in
